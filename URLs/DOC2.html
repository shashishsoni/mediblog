Deliver smarter patient care with integrated patient records

The Data Deluge in Healthcare: How OpenText Documentum Offers a Solution The healthcare industry faces a tidal wave of data. Patient information goes far beyond traditional Electronic Medical Records (EMRs). Emails, letters, referrals, images, and even handwritten notes contribute to a vast trove of unstructured data. According to the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center, a staggering 80% of a patient's medical record can be unstructured [1]. This valuable information often resides in isolated silos, hindering care delivery and frustrating both patients and providers. The Struggles of Disparate Data The current state of healthcare data management presents several challenges: Limited Accessibility: A significant portion of patient information remains inaccessible, locked away in disparate systems. Fax machines, a relic of the past, are still a dominant communication method between providers and payers. This fragmented data makes it difficult for healthcare professionals to get a complete picture of a patient's medical history, potentially leading to misdiagnosis or delayed treatment. Silos of Legacy Data: Legacy systems and archived data often contain valuable insights, but they are often isolated and inaccessible in their current form. This untapped information could be crucial for understanding a patient's long-term health trends and risk factors. Incomplete Patient View: The fragmented nature of data prevents healthcare providers from forming a comprehensive view of the patient. Social determinants of health, crucial factors influencing overall well-being, often remain outside the traditional EMR scope. OpenText Documentum for Healthcare: Bridging the Gaps OpenText Documentum for Healthcare tackles these challenges head-on by offering an integrated patient record solution. This platform empowers healthcare institutions to transform the way they manage, access, and share patient information, ultimately optimizing care delivery across the continuum. Key Features and Benefits of Documentum for Healthcare: Uniting Fragmented Data: Documentum bridges the gaps between disparate systems. It integrates data from legacy EMRs, paper records, and social determinants of health data. This allows healthcare providers to create a unified and patient-centric view of all essential information, regardless of source, location, or format. This holistic view empowers better-informed decisions and improved patient outcomes. Digitizing Patient Documents: Documentum facilitates the digitization of all patient documents, including medical images. This eliminates the need for paper records and ensures all information is readily available in a secure and easily accessible electronic format. Additionally, Documentum integrates clinical media seamlessly within clinical applications, streamlining the workflow for healthcare professionals. Enhanced Care Team Collaboration: Improved access to patient information is crucial for effective care coordination. Documentum provides a secure platform for authorized healthcare professionals within the organization to access all patient information at the point of care. This fosters collaboration among the care team, leading to more efficient and patient-centered care. Compliance and Secure Information Sharing: Documentum ensures secure information sharing within and beyond healthcare organizations. The platform adheres to Integrating the Healthcare Enterprise (IHE) standards, which govern secure data exchange across different healthcare systems. This allows for seamless information sharing while upholding patient privacy regulations. The Road to a More Efficient and Patient-Centered Healthcare System By leveraging OpenText Documentum for Healthcare, healthcare organizations can unlock the true potential of their vast patient data. A unified and easily accessible patient record empowers informed decision-making, improves care coordination, and ultimately leads to better patient outcomes. In a data-driven healthcare landscape, Documentum offers a powerful solution for navigating the data deluge and transforming the way we deliver care.
